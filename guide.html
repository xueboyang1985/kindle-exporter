📖 Kindle Highlights Exporter Guide

Export your Kindle highlights and notes to Markdown, CSV, or JSON — 100% in your browser.

How to Use

Step 1: Connect your Kindle to computer via USB
Step 2: Find the file Kindle/documents/My Clippings.txt on your Kindle
Step 3: Open Kindle Highlights Exporter in your browser, drag & drop the file onto the upload area
Step 4: Click Export as Markdown (or CSV / JSON) to download your highlights

Troubleshooting

No highlights found

Make sure you're using the correct My Clippings.txt file from your Kindle's documents folder. The file should contain your highlights and notes separated by ========== markers.

Export button doesn't download

Some browsers may block downloads. Look for a download icon in your browser's address bar and allow the download.

PRO key not working

Make sure you enter the key exactly as shown, with hyphens. Format: KINDLE-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX. If it still doesn't work, contact support with your purchase receipt.

How do I get My Clippings.txt?

Connect your Kindle to your computer via USB. It appears as a external drive. Navigate to the documents folder and look for My Clippings.txt. Copy it to your computer and drag it onto the tool.

If you don't have a Kindle, click "Load Sample Clippings" to try the tool with sample data.
